Get started

Your purchase includes both platform builds and two license activations.

  1. Open your Lemon Squeezy order and download the correct platform file.
  2. On macOS, open the DMG and move Scale and Cut 3D into Applications. On Windows, extract the entire ZIP before opening the EXE.
  3. Launch the app and enter the license key from your Lemon Squeezy receipt. Activation requires an internet connection.
  4. Install OrcaSlicer or Bambu Studio if one is not already installed.
  5. Choose a model, target size, printer, project format, and connector settings.
  6. Cut the model, review the exploded result, then generate plated files.

System requirements

Windows 10 or newer on x64 hardware, or macOS 12 or newer on Apple silicon. Plate generation requires OrcaSlicer or Bambu Studio.
